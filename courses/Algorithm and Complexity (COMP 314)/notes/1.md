# Summation formulae

## arithmetic sum
$$
	S(n) = n \frac{n+1}{2} 
$$
## geometric sum
$$
	G(n) = \frac{ x^{n+1} - 1 }{ x - 1 } 
$$
$$
	G(n) = \frac{ 1 }{ 1 - x } ,  where |x| < 1  
$$

# Sorting Algoritim

## selection sort 
```
	SelectionSort(A, n):
    	for(i = 1 to n-1)
        	m = i
            for(j = i+1 to n)
            	if(A[j] < A[m])
                	m = j
            temp = A[m]
            A[m] = A[i]
            A[i] = temp            
```

T(n) = $an^2 + bn + c$
Worst Case: $O(n^2)$
